<p>The family of four-year-old Mirabel Ilemoh, who was diagnosed with acute lymphoblastic leukemia (childhood cancer), is desperately seeking N5,914,610 million for treatment.</p>

<p>The treatment is for four-year of chemotherapy to save little Mirabel, who is currently in a sick bed.</p> 

<p>Research shows that acute lymphoblastic leukemia is a type of cancer of the blood and bone marrow that affects white blood cells.</p>

<p>It is the most common childhood cancer. It occurs when a bone marrow cell develops errors in its DNA.</p>

<p>Through a non-profit organization, Gifting Volunteer Network( Gvn), the family has called on the public to save their daughter.</p>

<p>The president of Gvn, Mr. Belele David. T told our reporter that Mirabel is among the many critical cases his organization has helped through public interventiontion </p>

<p>According to the medical report from Irrua Specialist Hospital in Benin Edo state, Mirabel was diagnosed on May 28th, 2023 with the disease.</p>

<p>Speaking to our correspondent, the patient's mother, Mrs Gloria Ilemoh, lamented her ordeal.</p> 

<p>"Mirabel is the first of two children. She has been in a sick bed for months now. It hurts to see your child suffer, and you can't help," she decried.</p>

<p>She continued that neither she nor her husband have the wherewithal to cater to the medical bill. "My husband earns N27,000 as a salesman in a company. He can't cater to this big amount."</p>

<p>Hence, on behalf of her family, Gloria appealed to all Nigerians to save her daughter from cancer of the bone and blood.</p>

<p>"Mirabel is one of the many cases Gifting Volunteer Network have handled over the years," This little girl is suffering and needs urgent help.</p>
