Who Are We?

FREE HEALTHCARE SERVICES FOR THOSE WHO CANNOT AFFORD

Gifting Volunteer Network is the largest technology platform for crowdfunding in the healthcare and Educational sector of Nigeria. It offers a complete range of free healthcare services including medical and surgical treatments, medical camps, and telehealth facilities to the underprivileged people of Nigeria. The platform provides visibility of needy patients and builds a personal and trusted bond between patients and donors while ensuring complete transparency. It also sets up free educational campaigns for those who can’t afford educational incentives in remote areas of Nigeria through public donations in which free textbooks, free exercise books, and other educational materials are provided to the deserving students.

WHERE IS THE PROBLEM?

More than 43 percent of Nigerians (89 million people) live below the poverty line, while another 25 percent (53 million) are vulnerable line due to which they are unable to undergo proper health treatment if they suffer from any disease. The estimated number of surgery backlogs in Nigeria is in millions every year.

  • There are not many health insurance plans for the underprivileged people or communities due to which, majority of the people are unable to undergo medical or surgical treatment.
  • Beyond the cost of medical and surgical procedures, they have to pay for other expenses such as pre and post-operative expenses which include; medical tests, medications, and hospital stay. It is almost impossible for someone who earns less than $5 a day to pay a hefty amount for medical relief.
  • There is a lack of medical facilities and medical professionals in rural and Urban areas across Nigeria due to which, poverty-stricken families are unable to avail required treatment.

GIFTING VOLUNTEER NETWORK ROLE IN NIGERIA
COMMITTED TO PROVIDE FREE HEALTHCARE SERVICES TO PEOPLE IN NEED ACROSS NIGERIA

Gifting Volunteer Network is a non-profit organization that is tackling this major healthcare issue in Nigeria through its unique Crowdfunding platform.
It is registered under Corporate Affairs Commission in Nigeria. Gifting Volunteer Network provides financial assistance to the people who can’t afford their medical bills across Nigeria in private and Public hospitals.
It also sets up free educational campaigns for children whose parents can’t afford their educational materials in which free textbooks, free exercise books, and other educational incentives are given to the needy.
Gifting Volunteer Network also publish his health stories on national dailies for those seeking for health assistance.
Donors from all over the world can use our online Crowdfunding web portal and donate through 100% secure payment modes. They could select any patient, donate for the treatment and receive regular feedback and updates until the patient is completely recovered.
Our user-friendly online fundraising platform works as follows:

  • Select a campaign that you want to support.
  • Choose from a number of payment methods.
  • Donate any amount.
  • Get regular updates of the patient until he/she gets completely recovered.
What makes us unique is; we ensure patients funds are monitored and disbursed as at when due.
Through our 100% transparency model, donors are ensured that each penny they donate is spent on needy patients. We do this by uploading all the hospital bills and other documents of our patient after his/her surgery are successfully conducted or he/she undergoes complete medical treatment. Donor can see the exact amount that has been charged against the treatment of that particular patient for which he has donated.

OUR IMPACT ON THE NIGERIA COMMUNITY

Gifting Volunteer Network is currently dealing in a number of surgeries including Hole in the heart(VSD), Cancer, Heart Defect, and other general surgeries.
It is striving to fund all cases before us. Gifting Volunteer Network is focusing on reaching out to the masses throughout the country who don’t have access to basic healthcare facilities by setting up free medical camps. It intends to expand to other areas of Africa where the poverty-stricken community has no means of getting financial assistance for surgeries through public patronage.
It is crucial to rescue those who has lost hope getting medical assistance among our underprivileged people regarding the most prevalent diseases. They need to know the circumstances of not treating a particular disease and what’d happen if they delay it for some reason. Not only this, people must have knowledge about symptoms of most common diseases including kidney stones, bladder stones, fibroid in women, hernia, and heart defects.
The mission of Gifting Volunteer Network is to give financial support to those who can’t afford their medical bills of all ages without any discrimination.
